About Caledon State Park

Caledon State Park is a scenic park along the Potomac River, featuring mature forests and river access. It's notable for bald eagles, woodpeckers, and seasonal migrants. The park's trails offer excellent opportunities for birding in a variety of habitats throughout the year.
