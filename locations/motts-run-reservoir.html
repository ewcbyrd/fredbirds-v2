About Motts Run Reservoir

Motts Run Reservoir is a 860-acre natural area offering a mix of water, forest, and meadow habitats. The reservoir and surrounding trails are excellent for spotting waterfowl, woodland birds, and seasonal migrants. Notable species include Wood Ducks, various warblers, and wintering ducks. The trail system provides access to diverse habitats for year-round birding.
