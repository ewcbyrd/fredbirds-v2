About Aquia Landing Park

Aquia Landing Park is a scenic waterfront park along Aquia Creek, offering excellent opportunities for waterfowl, waders, and migrating songbirds. The trail system winds through diverse habitats from creek edge to wooded areas. Birders can expect to see herons, egrets, ospreys, and a variety of songbirds, especially during migration.
