About Chancellorsville Battlefield

Chancellorsville Battlefield offers a mix of forest edge and open areas, making it excellent for cavity nesters, woodpeckers, and Eastern Bluebirds. The historic landscape is also home to a variety of warblers and other songbirds, especially during migration. Trails and open fields provide great opportunities for birding and history alike.
