About Crow's Nest Natural Area

Crow's Nest Natural Area Preserve features river access, mature forests, and wetlands. It's notable for neotropical migrants, resident forest birds, and seasonal waterfowl. The preserve's trails and boardwalks offer excellent birding opportunities throughout the year.
